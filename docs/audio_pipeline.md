# BloxChain Audio Pipeline

- Runtime music files are under `res://Audio/Music/`.
- Runtime SFX files are under `res://Audio/SFX/` and theme SFX are referenced through `ThemeManager.GENERAL_SFX_MAP` or theme config resources.
- Backup files such as `*.bak`, raw captures, and scratch audio must not ship.
- Music budget, SFX budget, and current measured sizes are recorded in `docs/release_packaging_checklist.md`.
