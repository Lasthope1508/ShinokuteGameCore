This file lists every third-party asset shipped with the template and the
license under which it is included. Buyers using the template are
responsible for keeping these credits whenever they ship a game built on
top of it (the licenses below all permit commercial and modified use,
some of them require attribution — keep this file in your project's
credits screen / about page when in doubt).

## Audio

* Audio files (`Audio/SFX/*.wav`, `Audio/Music/*.ogg`) supplied with the
  template are placeholders. Replace with your own licensed audio. If
  any of the bundled SFX come from a free-sound library, list the
  attribution requirements here before shipping your game.

---

## When you ship your game

1. Replace any placeholder icons / audio you don't have rights to
   redistribute.
2. Add an "About" / "Credits" screen mentioning at minimum the Alegreya
   font (it's an OFL requirement to credit the original designer in your
   project, even if the font is embedded into the binary).
3. Keep `LICENSE` and `OFL.txt` somewhere in the shipped package (the
   project root or a "/legal" folder is fine — both Godot's `user://`
   and your store listing's "credits" textarea satisfy this).
